This page lists all the different species and their abilities at level 1 that differentiate them from another. Some of these are relevant for the [general character creation](/) and [classes](/classes/).

There are 10 different species in the Dungeons and Dragons 5e 2024 Player Handbook. All of them have the creature type 'humanoid':

### Aasimar
> Size: `medium (about 4-7 feet tall) or small (about 2-4 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `celestial resistance, healing hands, light bearer, heavenly wings, inner radiance, necrotic shoud`

### Dragonborn
> Size: `medium (about 5-7 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `breath wapon, damage resistance, darkvision`

### Dwarf
> Size: `medium (about 4-5 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `darkvision, dwarven resilience, dwarven toughness, stonecunning`

### Elf
> Size: `medium (about 5-6 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `darkvision, elven lineage, fey ancestry, keen senses, trance`

### Gnome
> Size: `small (about 3-4 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `darkvision, gnomish cunning, gnomish lineage`

### Goliath
> Size: `medium (about 7-8 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `35 feet`
>
> Special traits: `giant ancesty, powerful build`

### Halfling
> Size: `small (about 2-3 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `brave, halfling nimbleness, luck, naturally stealthy`

### Human
> Size: `medium (about 4-7 feet tall) or small (about 2-4 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `resourceful, skillful, versatile`

### Orc
> Size: `medium (about 6-7 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `adrenaline rush, darkvision, relentless endurance`

### Tiefling
> Size: `medium (about 4-7 feet tall) or small (about 3-4 feet tall)`
>
> Speed: `30 feet`
>
> Special traits: `darkvision, fiendish legacy, otherworldly precence`
