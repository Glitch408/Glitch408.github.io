 This page lists all the different species and their abilities  at level 1 that differentiate them from another. Some of these are relevant for the [general character creation](/) and [species](/species/).

There are 12 different classes in the Dungeons and Dragons 5e 2024 Player Handbook: 

### Barbarian
> Primary ability: `strength`
>
> Hit point die: `d12`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `strength and constitution`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `animal handling, athletics, intimidation, nature, perception, survival`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple and martial weapons`
>
> Armor training: `light and medium armor and shields`
>
> Starting equipment: either `greataxe, 4 handaxes, explorer’s pack, 15 GP` or `75 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `rage, unarmored defence, weapon mastery`

### Bard
> Primary ability: `charisma`
>
> Hit point die: `d8`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `dexterity and charisma`
>
> Skill proficiencies: `3 of any skills`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple weapons`
>
> Tool proficiencies: `3 musical instruments`
>
> Armor training: `light armor`
>
> Starting equipment: either `leather armor, 2 daggers, musical instrument, entertainer's pack, 19 GP` or `90 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `bardic inspiration, spellcasting`

### Cleric
> Primary ability: `wisdom`
>
> Hit point die: `d8`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `wisdom and charisma`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `history, insight, medicine, persuasion, religion`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple weapon`
>
> Armor training: `light and medium armor and shields`
>
> Starting equipment: either `chain shirt, shield, mace, holy symbol, priest's pack, 7 GP` or `110 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `spellcasting, divine order`

### Druid
> Primary ability: `wisdom`
>
> Hit point die: `d8`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `intelligence and wisdom`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `arcana, animal handling, insight, medicine, nature, perception, religion, survival`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple weapons`
>
> Tool proficiencies: `herbalism kit`
>
> Armor training: `light armor and shields`
>
> Starting equipment: either `leather armor, shield, sickle, druidic focus (quarterstaff), explorer's pack, herbalism kit, 9 GP` or `50 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `spellcasting, druidic, primal order`

### Fighter
> Primary ability: `strength or dexterity`
>
> Hit point die: `d10`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `strength and constitution`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `acrobatics, animal handling, athletics, history, insight, intimidation, persuasion, perception, survival`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple and martial weapons`
>
> Armor training: `light, medium and heavy, armor and shields`
>
> Starting equipment: either `chain mail, greatsword, flail, 8 javelins, dungeoneer's pack, 4 GP` or `studded leather armor, scimitar, shortsword, longbow, 20 arrows, quiver, dungeoneer's pack, 11 GP` or `155 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `fighting style, second wind, weapon mastery`

### Monk
> Primary ability: `dexterity and wisdom`
>
> Hit point die: `d8`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `strenth and dexterity`
>
> Tool proficiencies: `one type of artisan's tools or musical instrument`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple weapons and martial weapons that have the light property`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `acrobatics, athletics, history, insight, religion, stealth`
>
> Armor training: `none`
>
> Starting equipment: either `spear, 5 daggers, artisan's tools, the item you have tool proficiency in, Explorer's Pack, 11 GP` or `50 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `martial arts, unarmored defence`

### Paladin
> Primary ability: `strengh and charisma`
>
> Hit point die: `d10`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `wisdom and charisma`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `athletics, insight, intimidation, medicine, persuasion, religion`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple and martial weapons`
>
> Armor training: `light, medium and heavy armor and shields`
>
> Starting equipment: either `chain mail, shield, longsword, 6 javelins, holy symbol, priest's pack, 9 GP` or `150 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `lay on hands, spellcasting, weapon mastery`

### Ranger
> Primary ability: `dexterity and wisdom`
>
> Hit point die: `d10`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `strenght and dexterity`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 3 of: `animal handling, athletics, insight, investigation, nature, perception, stealth, survival`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple and martial weapons`
>
> Armor training: `light and medium armor and shields`
>
> Starting equipment: either `studded leather armor, scimitar, shortsword, longbow, 20 arrows, quiver, druidic focus (sprig of mistletoe), explorer's pack, 7GP` or `150 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `spellcasting, favored enemy, weapon mastery`

### Rogue 
> Primary ability: `dexterity`
>
> Hit point die: `d8`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `dexterity and intelligence`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 4 of: `acrobatics, athletics, deception, insight, intimidation, investigation, perception, perception, persuasion, sleight of hand, stealth`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple weapons and martial weapons` that have the `finesse` or `light` property
>
> Tool proficiencies: `thieves' tools`
>
> Armor training: `light armor`
>
> Starting equipment: either `leather armor, 2 daggers, shortsword, shortbow, 20 arrows, quiver, thieves' tools, burglar's Pack, 8 GP` or `100 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `expertise, sneak attack, thieves' cant, weapon mastery`

### Sorcerer
> Primary ability: `charisma`
>
> Hit point die: `d6`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `constitution and charisma`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `arcana, deception, insight, intimidation, persuasion, religion`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple weapons`
>
> Armor training: `None`
>
> Starting equipment: either `spear, 2 daggers, arcane focus (crystal). dungeoneer's pack, 28 GP` or `50 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `spellcastion, innate sorcery`

### Warlock
> Primary ability: `charisma`
>
> Hit point die: `d8`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `wisdom and charisma`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `arcana, deception, history, intimidation, investigation, nature, religion`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple weapons`
>
> Armor training: `light armor`
>
> Starting equipment: either `leather armor, sicle, 2 daggers, arcane focus (orb), book (occult lore), scolar's pack, 15 GP` or `100 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `eldritch invocation, pact magic`

### Wizard
> Primary ability: `intelligence`
>
> Hit point die: `d6`
>
> Saving throw proficiencies: `intelligence and wisdom`
>
> Skill proficiencies: 2 of: `arcana, history, insight, investigation, medicine, nature, religion`
>
> Weapon proficiencies: `simple weapon`
>
> Armor training: `none`
>
> Starting equipment: either `2 daggers, arcane focus (quarterstaff), robe, spellbool, scolar's pack, 5 GP` or `55 GP`
>
> Level 1 features: `spellcasting, ritual adept, arcane recovery`
